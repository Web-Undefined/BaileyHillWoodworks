Millwork

Fascinated by the lasting natural beauty of wood windows and doors, Bailey Hill Woodworks is equipped with the tools and knowledge to replicate and repair antique windows and doors using traditional joinery. Wood windows are more energy efficient in the sense that when taken care of correctly they will outlast plastic windows tenfold. Before making the decision to replace wood windows on your antique home or barn with vinyl, give us a call to discuss restoration and reproduction options.
