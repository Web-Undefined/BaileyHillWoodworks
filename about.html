About

CARPENTRY AND WOODWORKING SERVICES WITHOUT BOUNDARIES

Bailey Hill Woodworks

Surrounded by New England’s beautiful antique homes, our shop is nestled in Connecticut’s quiet corner. With over 15 years’ experience, owner Kevin O’Keefe’s passion and growing knowledge for pre-industrial New England woodworking and handicraft has inspired him to strive at keeping quality craftsmanship in carpentry and woodworking alive and well.
