Services

Specializing in all aspects of carpentry and woodworking, Bailey Hill Woodworks offers its services to Rhode Island, and Connecticut’s quiet corner. In this sped up world of mass production we focus on reviving quality over quantity. We encourage the use of locally sourced lumber and environmentally friendly building products.
